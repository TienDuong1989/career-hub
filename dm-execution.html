Link Building

What is Link Building?

Getting other websites to link to your pages. Each backlink = a "vote of trust" that increases Domain Rating (DR) and helps pages rank higher. For B2B IT outsourcing, focus on industry directories, guest posts, and PR mentions.

Tactic Type Effort Impact Notes Status
Guest posting Outbound High High Write for tech/IT blogs in target market
Directory listings Inbound Low Medium Clutch, GoodFirms, Upwork profile
PR / media mentions Outbound High High Press releases, award submissions
Broken link building Outbound Medium Medium Find broken links on target sites, offer replacement
Case study co-publish Inbound Medium High Client agrees to publish case study on their site
Outbound You push to audience. Pay per click or impression. Fast results, stops when budget stops.

Google Ads — Search (High Intent)

Target people actively searching for IT outsourcing services. Best for Convert stage keywords. Set up exact match + phrase match campaigns, negative keyword list, and track CPL by campaign.

LinkedIn Ads — ICP Targeting

Target by job title (CTO, VP Engineering, IT Director), industry, and company size. Best for Engage and Convert stages. More expensive CPL but higher lead quality for B2B.

Both Serves all channels — inbound traffic from SEO/Content AND outbound traffic from Ads.

Lead Funnel / CVR Tracking

Track conversion rate (CVR) by page. Identify where visitors drop off in the funnel. Key metric: sessions → form view → form submit → qualified lead. Target CVR ≥ 1% on service/landing pages.

CRO — Conversion Rate Optimization

Systematic improvement of pages to increase CVR without increasing traffic. Tactics: clear CTA placement, form shortening, page speed, trust signals (client logos, case study links), A/B testing headlines.
